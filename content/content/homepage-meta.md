# Homepage Meta Description Recommendations

## Project
Sell My House Quickly UK

## Objective
Improve organic CTR, relevance, and AI search visibility by rewriting the homepage meta description to better match transactional search intent while maintaining natural language.

## Existing Issues

- Meta description lacks a strong value proposition.
- Limited focus on high-conversion search intent.
- Weak call-to-action.
- Opportunity to improve AI Overview and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) relevance.

## Recommended Meta Description

Sell your house quickly anywhere in the UK with trusted cash home buyers. Get a free no-obligation cash offer, avoid estate agent fees, and complete your sale fast with a simple and hassle-free process.

## SEO Focus

- Transactional Search Intent
- AI Search Optimization (AEO)
- Entity SEO
- Semantic SEO
- CTR Optimization
- Conversion Optimization

## Target Search Themes

- sell my house quickly
- sell house fast UK
- cash house buyers
- quick house sale
- sell property fast
- no estate agent fees
